Questions

Common questions you might have about the wedding week, ceremony or reception.

Can I bring a date?

We are only able to accommodate a specific number of guests. Those who have been given a plus one will know from their formal invitation.

Are children welcome?

Although we love your kids, this is an adults-only celebration.

Is there parking/transportation provided?

There will be complimentary valet parking at the event for those driving. For those staying in the area, we will provide an Uber voucher to help with your travels.

What is black tie?

Men should opt for a dark tuxedo, or a dark suit with necktie or bow tie if preferred. Women should wear a full-length formal dress.

What kind of food and drinks will be served?

You will be shown your options when we send out formal invites during the RSVP step. There will be an open bar.

Is there a hotel block?

We are working on finalizing a hotel block for guests and we will update the site accordingly.
